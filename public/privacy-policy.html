1. Introduction

Servigic ("we," "our," "us") is a home services marketplace platform that connects Customers who need home repair and maintenance work with verified independent Service Providers ("Pros"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect information when you use the Servigic mobile application (Customer App and Pro App) and related services.

By creating an account or using Servigic, you agree to the collection and use of information as described in this policy.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Account Information

  • Full name, phone number, and email address
  • Password (stored securely, hashed — never stored in plain text)
  • Profile photo (optional, for Service Providers a profile photo is required per our verification standards)
  • City / area of residence
  • Preferred language

2.2 Service Provider (Pro) Verification Information

  • CNIC (national identity card) copy, for identity verification
  • Selfie photo, for identity match verification
  • Trade/skill category (plumber, electrician, AC technician, carpenter, etc.)
  • Service area / radius
  • Payout account details (JazzCash, EasyPaisa, or bank account) — used only to send your earnings

2.3 Job & Booking Information

  • Job descriptions, categories, and photos you upload when posting a job
  • Bid amounts, messages, and offers exchanged between Customers and Pros
  • Booking status, timestamps, and job completion details
  • Ratings and reviews submitted after a completed booking
  • In-app chat messages related to an active booking

2.4 Payment Information

Servigic does not directly store your full card, bank, or wallet credentials. Payments are processed through licensed third-party payment processors (such as Safepay, JazzCash, or EasyPaisa). We retain only transaction references, amounts, and status needed to confirm and reconcile bookings.

2.5 Device & Usage Information

  • Device type, operating system, and app version
  • IP address and general (country/city-level) location derived from it
  • App usage analytics (screens viewed, features used, crash reports)
  • Push notification tokens (to deliver job alerts and status updates)

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your Customer or Pro account
  • To match job requests with nearby, relevant Service Providers
  • To send real-time notifications — new job alerts, bid updates, booking status, and dispatch alerts
  • To process payments and release payouts to Service Providers
  • To verify Service Provider identity and maintain platform trust and safety
  • To enable in-app chat between a Customer and Pro on a confirmed booking
  • To display relevant advertising (via Google AdMob) to support free use of the app
  • To improve app performance, fix bugs, and develop new features
  • To detect fraud, abuse, and violations of our Terms of Service
  • To respond to customer support requests

4. Location Data

Why we need it: Location access is core to how Servigic works — it lets us match your job with nearby Service Providers, show your Pro's live location while they're on the way to you, and calculate accurate service areas.

  • Customers: approximate location is used to post a job and match nearby Pros. Your exact address is only shared with a Provider after a booking is confirmed and paid for.
  • Service Providers: location is used to receive relevant job alerts within your service radius, and is shared with the Customer only while a booking is active and you have marked yourself "On the way."

You can disable location permissions in your device settings at any time, but doing so will limit or disable core app functionality (job posting, dispatch, and live tracking).

5. Advertising (Google AdMob)

Third-Party Advertising

Servigic uses Google AdMob to display advertisements within the app. AdMob may collect and use certain data to show ads, including:

  • Advertising ID (a resettable, non-permanent device identifier)
  • General device information and app usage data
  • Approximate location (if location permission is granted)

This data is used by Google to serve relevant ads and measure ad performance. We do not control how Google uses this data beyond our integration; Google's use of advertising data is governed by the Google Ads and Content Network Privacy Policy and Google Privacy Policy.

You can opt out of personalized advertising by adjusting your device's ad settings (Settings → Google → Ads → "Opt out of Ads Personalization" on Android) or by resetting your Advertising ID.

7. Data Sharing & Disclosure

We do not sell your personal information. We share information only in these circumstances:

  • Between Customers and Pros: name (first name only, pre-booking), rating, job details, and — after a booking is confirmed and paid — contact details and address, so the job can be completed.
  • With service providers listed in Section 6, strictly to operate the app.
  • For legal reasons: if required by law, court order, or to protect the rights, safety, or property of Servigic, our users, or the public.
  • Business transfers: if Servigic is involved in a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, user information may be transferred as part of that transaction, subject to the same privacy protections.

8. Data Security

We use industry-standard measures to protect your data, including encrypted data transmission (HTTPS/TLS), hashed passwords, and role-based access controls limiting who within our team can access user data. However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

9. Data Retention

We retain account and booking information for as long as your account is active, and for a reasonable period afterward to comply with legal, tax, and dispute-resolution obligations. Live location data shared during an active booking (Section 4) is retained only briefly and is not kept once a booking is completed. You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time (see Section 10).

10. Your Rights & Choices

  • Access & correction: you can view and update your profile information directly within the app.
  • Account deletion: you may request full deletion of your account and personal data by contacting us at the email below.
  • Notification preferences: you can manage push notification permissions in your device settings.
  • Location permissions: can be revoked at any time in device settings (with the functionality limitations noted in Section 4).
  • Ad personalization: can be limited via device-level ad settings as described in Section 5.

11. Children's Privacy

Servigic is not directed at children under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will take steps to delete it.
