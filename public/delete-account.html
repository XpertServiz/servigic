Delete Your Account

App: Servigic  |  Package name: com.xpertserviz.servigic  |  Developer: Muhammad Ather Khan  |  Last updated: July 2026

You can request permanent deletion of your Servigic account and associated personal data at any time. This applies to both Customer and Service Provider (Pro) accounts. Please read the steps and data-retention details below before submitting your request.

How to Request Account Deletion

1
Send a deletion request email

Email xpertserviz@gmail.com with the subject line: "Servigic Account Deletion Request."

2
Include your account details

In the email, include your registered phone number or email address used to sign up on Servigic, and whether your account is a Customer account, a Pro (Service Provider) account, or both. This is required so we can locate and verify the correct account.

3
Identity confirmation

For security, we may reply asking you to confirm your identity (for example, by verifying an OTP sent to your registered number) before processing the deletion — this protects you from someone else requesting deletion of your account.

4
Confirmation & processing time

Once verified, your account and eligible data will be deleted within 7–14 business days. You'll receive a confirmation email once deletion is complete.

Alternative: Logged-in users can also request deletion directly from the app: go to Account → Settings → Delete My Account, which submits the same request automatically with your account details pre-filled.

What Data Is Deleted vs. Retained

When your account deletion is processed, we handle your data as follows:

Data TypeWhat Happens
Profile information (name, photo, phone, email, address) Deleted
Account login credentials Deleted
Job posts, bids, and in-app chat messages Deleted (or anonymized if linked to another user's active/completed booking)
Service Provider verification documents (CNIC, selfie) Deleted
Ratings and reviews you left for others Deleted or anonymized so the review record isn't unfairly removed from the other party
Completed transaction & payment records Retained — required for tax, accounting, and legal compliance purposes for a limited retention period, even after account deletion
Dispute or fraud-prevention records (if applicable) Retained — kept only as necessary to comply with legal obligations or resolve an open dispute
Note: If you have an active, unpaid, or disputed booking at the time of your request, we may ask you to resolve or wait for that booking to close before we can complete full account deletion, to protect the other party involved (Customer or Pro) in that transaction.
